 New Security Features
What is the new security feature?

Users of online banking have always authenticated themselves to gain access. From now on, online banking will reciprocate and will also authenticate itself to you.

You will know with every certainty that you are visiting online banking because you will be presented a graphic and phrase each time you logon to Rutgers FCU.

Has my password changed?

No. The answer you provide to the "challenge question" is separate from your regular online banking login password.
 
A User ID and Password is required to log in to our services. Services are provided through a secure connection. If you have difficulty logging in please use the Forgot your password link, call Credit Union at (732)932-7645 or email us at info@rutgersfcu.org -DO NOT send personally identifying information (i.e. Acct #, SS #, etc) in your email.

Unauthorized attempts to access, upload information, or deface this web site is strictly prohibited and are subject to prosecution under the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act of 1986 and Title 18 U.S.C. Sec. 1001 and 1030.
